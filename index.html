Welcome to the OBS counter widget!
This page will display a number for five seconds and hide it afterwards.
It can be useful for counting some events that occur during a stream.

You may use this with a traditional browser and a green-screen.
You may also add this to OBS using the Browser source.
After that, right-click the source and choose "Interact" to open a mini-browser.
Left-click anywhere in the browser window to increase the counter by 1.
Right-click to assign a custom value to the counter.
The counter value persists accross browser/OBS restarts.
Do either of those now to hide these instructions. They will never be shown again.
Need multiple counters? Add ?counter=some_name_here to the end of the URL.
